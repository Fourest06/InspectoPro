Inspection & Test Plan Checklist

# Question Yes No Remarks
Project Quality Management System
1 Is the project quality management system established and documented?
2 Are quality objectives defined and communicated?
3 Are quality responsibilities and authorities defined and assigned?
4 Is the quality management system reviewed and updated regularly?
Management Responsibility
1 Is top management committed to the quality management system?
2 Are quality policies and objectives communicated to all personnel?
3 Are quality responsibilities and authorities defined and assigned?
4 Is management review of the quality management system conducted regularly?
5 Are corrective actions taken to address quality issues?
Resource Management
1 Are personnel with necessary skills and training assigned to quality-related tasks?
2 Are resources (e.g., equipment, materials) provided to support quality activities?
3 Are quality-related documents and records maintained and controlled?
4 Are internal audits conducted to ensure compliance with quality requirements?
5 Are corrective actions taken to address quality issues?
6 Are quality records maintained and retained?
7 Are personnel aware of their quality responsibilities?
8 Is training provided to personnel on quality-related topics?
Measurements, Analysis, and Improvement
1 Are quality metrics and performance indicators established and tracked?
2 Are data analysis and reporting conducted to identify quality trends?
3 Are corrective actions taken to address quality issues?
4 Are quality improvement initiatives implemented and tracked?
5 Are lessons learned documented and applied to future projects?
6 Is the quality management system continuously improved?
Construction Inspection Points
01. QC Application for Inspection
1 Has the QC application for inspection been completed?
2 Are all required inspection documents attached?
3 Has the inspection request been approved?
02. Non-conformance
1 Have non-conformities been identified and documented?
2 Have corrective actions been taken to address non-conformities?
3 Have non-conformities been verified as closed?
03. Project Quality Audits
1 Have project quality audits been conducted?
2 Have audit findings been documented and addressed?
3 Have corrective actions been taken to address audit findings?
4 Have audit findings been verified as closed?
5 Are audit results communicated to relevant personnel?
6 Are audit results used to improve the quality management system?
7 Are audit schedules and plans established and followed?
04. Demolition and Salvage
1 Has a demolition and salvage plan been developed?
2 Are demolition and salvage activities conducted safely?
3 Are demolition and salvage materials properly disposed of?

5 Are demolition and salvage activities documented?
6 Are demolition and salvage personnel trained and qualified?
7 Are demolition and salvage equipment properly maintained?
