Quality Control QC Procedure

# Question Yes No Remarks
QC Planning and Documentation
1Is there an approved Quality Control Plan (QCP) specific to the project?
2Is the QCP aligned with the DPWH Blue Book and contract specifications?
3Are the quality objectives, KPIs, and procedures clearly defined?
4Is there a designated Quality Control Manager or Engineer assigned?
5Has the QCP been reviewed and accepted by the Engineer/Client?
6Are method statements and inspection test plans (ITPs) available and updated?
7Are daily QC reports, punch lists, and inspection logs maintained and filed?
8Is there a documented procedure for handling non-conformance and corrective actions?
Materials Inspection and Testing
1Are incoming materials inspected and recorded before acceptance on-site?
2Are Material Approval Submittals (MAS) filed and approved before use?
3Are material test results (e.g., concrete compressive strength, soil compaction) available and within required specifications?
4Are third-party testing laboratories accredited by DPWH or recognized agencies?
5Are batching, mixing, and curing processes in compliance with standards?
6Are rejected materials properly tagged, documented, and segregated?
7Is there a calibration log for all QC testing equipment?
8Are testing frequencies in accordance with project specifications or DPWH guidelines?
9Are stockpiles and storage areas organized to prevent contamination or damage?
10Are test results submitted regularly to the supervising consultant or client?
Workmanship and Site Execution
1Are construction activities carried out in accordance with approved shop drawings and specifications?
2Are field inspections conducted before, during, and after major activities (e.g., concreting, backfilling)?
3Are skilled workers certified or adequately trained for specialized work (e.g., welders, equipment operators)?
4Are quality checkpoints being conducted and documented by the QC team?
5Are safety and quality integrated during site inspections?
6Are NCRs issued for observed defects and reworks tracked to completion?
7Are corrective and preventive actions implemented and verified?
8Are there recurring quality issues, and how are they being addressed?
9Are there clear acceptance criteria for major work items (e.g., pavement, structural elements)?
10Are mock-ups or first work inspections conducted as required?
Subcontractor and Supplier Quality Compliance
1Are subcontractors required to submit their own QCP or comply with the main contractor's plan?
2Are suppliers and subcontractors evaluated for quality performance before engagement?
3Are deliveries from suppliers documented with compliance certificates and test results?
4Are subcontractor works subjected to the same QC inspections and documentation?
5Are there site orientations or quality meetings held with subcontractors?
6Are penalties or rework procedures enforced for repeated quality failures?
7Are performance evaluations conducted and recorded for all subcontractors?
8Are there records of coordination meetings regarding quality and execution?
Inspection, Turnover, and Close-Out
1Is there a punch list system for pre-final inspections?
2Are inspections jointly conducted with the client/consultant and documented?
3Is there a clear timeline and responsibility matrix for turnover requirements?
4Are As-Built Drawings, test results, warranties, and O&M manuals prepared and compiled?
5Are final test results submitted and verified prior to handover?
6Has a final QC report or project close-out report been submitted?
7Is there a system for tracking post-construction defects (e.g., during DLP)?
8Are final inspections documented with client acceptance or approval?
