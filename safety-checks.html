Health, Safety & Environmental Check Sheets

# Question Yes No Remarks
HSE Check Sheet Design and Approval
1Are HSE check sheets tailored for specific construction activities (e.g., scaffolding, electrical works)?
2Are the check sheets reviewed and approved by the HSE Manager and Safety Officer?
3Do the check sheets incorporate Philippine laws such as RA 11058 and DOLE D.O. 13?
4Are there specific sections addressing environmental compliance (e.g., noise, dust, waste)?
5Do the check sheets include PPE requirements per activity?
6Is the latest revision of the check sheet being used on site?
7Are check sheets aligned with the Construction Safety and Health Program (CSHP)?
8Are daily safety checklists authorized by the assigned Safety Officer or Safety Inspector?
PPE, Worksite Safety, and Daily Implementation
1Is proper PPE for workers strictly implemented and documented on the check sheet?
2Are visitors and subcontractors also required to wear appropriate PPE?
3Are PPE items appropriate to the task (e.g., full-body harness for heights, cut-resistant gloves)?
4Are defective, damaged, or expired PPE replaced immediately?
5Are workers using additional PPE for high-risk tasks (e.g., welding mask, chemical goggles)?
6Are there daily inspections to verify PPE compliance before work starts?
7Are PPE violations recorded in the HSE check sheets and acted upon?
8Are safety signage, warning tapes, and barricades in place and noted in checklists?
9Are daily toolbox talks conducted and documented as part of the HSE check process?
10Are fall protection systems, scaffolding, and ladders inspected and recorded daily?
Monitoring and Documentation of HSE Check Sheets
1Are completed HSE check sheets submitted and filed daily or weekly?
2Are the findings from HSE check sheets used in weekly safety reports?
3Are HSE check sheet logs tagged by trade, activity, or subcontractor?
4Is there a follow-up system for unclosed items or recurring violations?
5Are PPE deficiencies, near-misses, or unsafe acts tracked over time using these forms?
6Are scanned or digital records of HSE check sheets maintained for audit purposes?
7Are environmental incidents (e.g., waste mismanagement, water runoff) documented in check sheets?
8Are results of HSE check sheets shared during coordination or toolbox meetings?
9Are incomplete, missing, or falsified check sheets reported?
10Are DOLE site visits or third-party safety inspections cross-referenced with check sheet records?
Corrective Actions and Hazard Mitigation
1Are corrective actions from check sheet observations implemented promptly?
2Is there documentation that unsafe behaviors (e.g., not wearing PPE) are corrected or sanctioned?
3Are hazard sources identified in check sheets mitigated (e.g., removing sharp objects, covering pits)?
4Are fire safety, electrical safety, and confined space entries monitored through check sheets?
5Are fall incidents or "almost-falls" documented and followed by safety refreshers?
6Are work stoppages due to serious violations recorded in the HSE check sheets?
7Are action plans for environmental non-conformance (e.g., improper disposal) followed through?
8Are updates to the CSHP made when recurring HSE issues are observed in check sheets?
9Is HSE team follow-up conducted for violations that persist across multiple checklists?
10Are signed validations required for completed or corrected findings?
Compliance Review and Continuous Improvement
1Are trends from HSE check sheets analyzed (e.g., frequent PPE violations)?
2Are HSE audit results cross-validated with data from check sheets?
3Are changes to safety protocols or PPE policies based on check sheet data?
4Are HSE check sheet formats reviewed periodically for relevance and effectiveness?
5Are workers involved in improving checklist content based on real site experience?
6Are compliance metrics (e.g., % PPE compliance, % closed hazards) reported monthly?
7Are good safety practices recognized in HSE committee meetings?
8Are environmental compliance (DENR permits, waste disposal practices) regularly reviewed via check sheets?
9Are DOLE or client audit findings used to update the HSE check sheets?
10Are check sheet data included in end-of-project HSE reports and lessons learned sessions?
